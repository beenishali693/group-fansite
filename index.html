A young wizard’s journey through magic, friendship, and adventure

The Harry Potter series, written by J.K. Rowling, chronicles the life and adventures of a young boy who discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard. Set in a magical world hidden within our own, the series spans seven books, each following Harry and his friends—Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley—through their years at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. As Harry grows, he learns about his past, uncovers secrets about his parents' mysterious deaths, and ultimately faces the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort, who seeks to conquer the wizarding world. Through themes of friendship, bravery, and the battle between good and evil, the series has captivated readers worldwide, inspiring generations and becoming a global cultural phenomenon.
