Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Harry discovers he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts, where he makes friends and enemies and uncovers a plot involving the powerful Sorcerer Stone. He learns of his connection to the dark wizard Voldemort, who killed his parents.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Mysterious attacks on students and a chilling message warn that the "Chamber of Secrets" has been opened, endangering Hogwarts. Harry, Ron, and Hermione investigate and discover a dark secret from the school's past.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

A dangerous prisoner, Sirius Black, escapes from Azkaban, allegedly seeking Harry. Harry learns about his parents' friends and confronts secrets that change his understanding of family and betrayal. With Hermione's help, Harry saves Sirius, who turns out to be his godfather and a friend.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Harry is unexpectedly entered into the dangerous Tri-wizard Tournament, facing deadly challenges. Along the way, he witnesses Voldemort's return to power during the final task.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

As the Ministry of Magic denies Voldemort's return, Harry and his friends form Dumbledore's Army to prepare for battle. Harry learns more about his own connection to Voldemort and faces loss and betrayal.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

Harry delves into Voldemort's past, uncovering secrets about his rise to power and how he can be defeated. He learns about Horcruxes, objects that contain pieces of Voldemort's soul.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

Harry, Ron, and Hermione set out to destroy Voldemort's Horcruxes, leading to a final showdown at Hogwarts. Harry faces his destiny in the fight against Voldemort, ultimately triumphing. Voldemort is defeated, and peace is restored to the wizarding world.
